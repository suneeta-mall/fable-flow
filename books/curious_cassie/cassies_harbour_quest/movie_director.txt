# Storyboard Animation Prompts for "Curious Cassie's Harbour Quest"

<storyboard>
Spectacular fountain of orange glittery foam erupts explosively from cup, bubbling and fizzing wildly. Cassie's face lights with pure excitement, eyes wide with wonder. Caleb bounces with delight, sticky hands clapping. Rainbow bubbles drift lazily through window. Kitchen counter splattered with colorful foam creating chaotic scientific joy.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Mum gestures warmly over baking ingredients spread across counter. Cassie and Caleb lean forward eagerly, eyes tracking between bowls and measuring tools. Natural light streams through window, illuminating flour particles floating in air. Their expressions show growing curiosity about the baking experiment ahead.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Family gathers around table examining two contrasting cakes. Cassie closes eyes thoughtfully, savoring taste with scientific concentration. Caleb grins widely, chocolate smeared on cheeks and chin. Light catches the different textures—one fluffy and airy, one dense and fudgy. Dad reaches for another slice.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Mum carefully places red and black fishing rod into car boot while Dad positions blue esky. Children's excited faces press against car window, eyes sparkling with anticipation. Morning sunlight creates warm glow. Parents exchange knowing glances about the surprise. Eager energy radiates from scene.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Vibrant fish swims in crystal-clear blue water, gills opening and closing rhythmically. Water flows visibly through gill structures with gentle current. Six distinct fins move independently—pectoral fins steering, caudal fin propelling. Bubbles rise slowly. The fish's body undulates gracefully demonstrating swimming mechanics.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Cassie and Caleb press faces against car windows, eyes widening with wonder. Sydney Harbour Bridge grows larger through windscreen, spanning sparkling blue water. Their expressions transform from anticipation to awe. Buildings and boats become clearer. Parents smile at children's excitement from front seats.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Cassie stands transfixed, breath caught in amazement at stunning harbour panorama. Her eyes slowly scan from gleaming Opera House to Luna Park's smiling face. Sailboats drift gracefully. Ferry creates v-shaped wake. Her expression radiates pure wonder. Family watches her reaction with warm smiles.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Cassie lowers water-filled bottle into clear container, concentration intense on her face. Bottle glug-glugs downward, sinking to bottom with visible bubbles. Empty bottle bobs happily on surface nearby. Caleb watches with wide-eyed fascination, gripping container edge. Understanding dawns on Cassie's face.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Cassie and Caleb leap upward with arms raised triumphantly, mouths open wide shouting "EUREKA!" Pure joy radiates from their animated expressions. Mum and Dad smile warmly at their enthusiasm. Other harbour visitors turn with amused grins. Massive cargo ship floats majestically behind them.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Crystal-clear rock pool teems with subtle movement. Small crab scuttles sideways across pebbles in distinctive shuffle. Seaweed sways gently in current. Purple sea star clings motionless to rock. Hermit crab emerges slightly from spiral shell. Cassie and Caleb peer down with wonder.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Sea anemone slowly unfurls delicate tentacles like flower petals opening in time-lapse. Soft appendages sway gracefully in gentle current. Fresh seawater sparkles washing over rocks. All four family members watch in reverent awe, faces reflecting amazement and connection. Caleb's hand held gently by Mum.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Caleb kneels beside beautiful silvery Yellowfin Bream on wooden wharf, face glowing with wonder and pride. Dad points to fish's distinct fins while explaining. Fish's gills open and close rhythmically. Cassie readies camera behind them. Harbour sparkles in background with seabirds wheeling overhead.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Marine biologists in wetsuits walk purposefully toward harbour water carrying scientific equipment, nets, and clipboards. Cassie and Caleb watch with inspired expressions, eyes following the scientists with admiration. Caleb clutches rainbow crayons and nature journal. Parents stand supportively behind children. Inspiration visible on young faces.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Cassie sleeps peacefully, gentle smile on face. Dream bubble above shows underwater scene with colorful fish swimming through sparkling blue water.
