    Community Guidelines ü§ù#

    Welcome to the FableFlow community! We're excited to have you join our growing family of storytellers, educators, and curious minds. To ensure everyone has a positive and inspiring experience, please follow these guidelines.

    üåü Our Values#

    Curiosity First#

    • Ask questions, explore ideas, and embrace the wonder of learning
    • Share discoveries and insights with others
    • Celebrate the joy of "not knowing yet" - it's where growth begins

    Inclusive Creativity#

    • Welcome storytellers of all backgrounds, skill levels, and perspectives
    • Respect diverse viewpoints and creative approaches
    • Support each other's learning and creative journeys

    Constructive Collaboration#

    • Provide helpful, specific feedback on stories and ideas
    • Share knowledge and resources generously
    • Lift others up rather than tear down

    Educational Excellence#

    • Prioritize accuracy when sharing educational content
    • Cite sources and give credit where due
    • Strive for age-appropriate, engaging content

    üí¨ Community Spaces#

    GitHub Discussions#

    Our primary community hub for:

    • Show & Tell: Share your FableFlow creations
    • Help & Support: Get assistance with technical questions
    • Feature Requests: Suggest improvements and new features
    • General Chat: Connect with other community members

    Blog Comments#

    Engage thoughtfully on our Curiosity Chronicles: - Share related experiences and insights - Ask follow-up questions - Suggest topics for future posts

    Story Feedback#

    When reviewing community stories: - Focus on what works well first - Offer specific, actionable suggestions - Consider the target audience and educational goals - Be encouraging and supportive

    üìù Content Guidelines#

    Story Submissions#

    Acceptable Content: - Original stories created with FableFlow - Educational and entertaining narratives - Age-appropriate content for specified audiences - Stories that inspire curiosity and learning

    Please Avoid: - Copyrighted material without proper attribution - Content that promotes harmful stereotypes - Stories with inappropriate language or themes - Purely promotional content without educational value

    Educational Standards#

    When creating educational content: - Verify scientific accuracy - Use reliable, credible sources - Adapt complexity to target age group - Include learning objectives when relevant

    üöÄ Getting Started in the Community#

    New Members#

    1. Introduce yourself in GitHub Discussions
    2. Explore existing stories to understand our style and quality
    3. Start small - try our beginner tutorials first
    4. Ask questions - we love helping new creators!

    Sharing Your Work#

    1. Test thoroughly before sharing
    2. Provide context - explain your story's purpose and audience
    3. Include educational notes if applicable
    4. Be open to feedback and iteration

    Contributing Code#

    If you're interested in technical contributions:

    1. Review our technical documentation
    2. Check existing issues and feature requests
    3. Follow our coding standards and testing requirements
    4. Submit pull requests with clear descriptions

    üõ°Ô∏è Community Standards#

    Respectful Communication#

    • Use welcoming, inclusive language
    • Disagree respectfully and constructively
    • Avoid personal attacks or discriminatory language
    • Stay on-topic in discussions

    Intellectual Property#

    • Respect copyrights and attribution requirements
    • Share only content you have rights to distribute
    • Give credit to original creators and sources
    • Use FableFlow's open-source nature responsibly

    Privacy & Safety#

    • Protect personal information (yours and others')
    • Don't share contact information publicly
    • Report concerning behavior to moderators
    • Keep children's safety paramount in educational content

    üéØ Quality Standards#

    Story Quality#

    We encourage high-quality contributions:

    • Clear narrative structure with beginning, middle, and end
    • Engaging characters that resonate with target audience
    • Educational value that enhances learning
    • Technical quality in multimedia elements

    Educational Value#

    Stories should contribute meaningfully to learning:

    • Align with educational standards when applicable
    • Include discussion questions or extension activities
    • Provide accurate, age-appropriate information
    • Inspire further exploration and curiosity

    üì¢ Communication Channels#

    For Help & Support#

    • Technical Issues: GitHub Issues
    • General Questions: GitHub Discussions
    • Story Feedback: Community showcase threads
    • Private Concerns: Direct email to maintainers

    For Contributions#

    • Story Submissions: Community showcase
    • Bug Reports: GitHub Issues
    • Feature Requests: GitHub Discussions
    • Code Contributions: Pull Requests

    üåà Celebrating Success#

    We love celebrating community achievements:

    • Creator Spotlights in our blog
    • Featured Stories in our library
    • Technical Contributions acknowledged in releases
    • Community Awards for outstanding contributions

    üîÑ Feedback & Improvement#

    These guidelines evolve with our community:

    • Suggest improvements via GitHub Discussions
    • Participate in community surveys and feedback sessions
    • Help us create a better experience for everyone

    üìû Contact & Reporting#

    Community Moderators#

    Our volunteer moderators help maintain a positive environment:

    • Monitor discussions for guideline adherence
    • Help resolve conflicts constructively
    • Connect community members with appropriate resources

    Reporting Issues#

    If you encounter problems:

    1. Technical Issues: Create a GitHub issue with details
    2. Community Concerns: Contact moderators privately
    3. Urgent Safety Issues: Email maintainers directly

    üéâ Welcome to the Adventure!#

    We're thrilled you're part of the FableFlow community. Whether you're here to:

    • Discover amazing stories that spark curiosity
    • Create your own adventures with AI assistance
    • Connect with other creators and educators
    • Contribute to the platform technically or creatively

    ...you're in the right place!

    Let's build something amazing together. üöÄ

    These guidelines are inspired by our commitment to fostering curiosity, creativity, and inclusive learning. Thank you for helping make FableFlow a welcoming space for all storytellers.
