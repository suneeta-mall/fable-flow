# Storyboard Animation Prompts for "Curious Cassie's Beach Ride Quest"

<storyboard>
Cassie sits peacefully in sandpit, fingers trailing through sparkling sand. She tilts head thoughtfully as tree branches sway gently overhead, casting dancing shadows across her sun-kissed face. Light breeze makes wattle blooms flutter. Sand cascades from her red spade in glittering streams. Her expression shifts from contentment to dawning curiosity.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Mother kneels on grass with children settled closely on either side like eager bookends. Both kids lean forward with upturned faces radiating wonder and anticipation. Mum's hands gesture expressively as she shares knowledge. Caleb clutches dinosaur tightly while Cassie's eyes shine with curiosity. Warm connection between all three, wattle tree swaying softly behind them.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Both children leap joyfully in mid-air, arms outstretched wide, faces glowing with discovery and delight. Sand particles and raindrops hang suspended around them, demonstrating gravity's pull. Cassie spins gracefully while Caleb bounces enthusiastically with dinosaur. Mum watches with loving smile, beach towel over arm. Golden light creates magical atmosphere of scientific wonder through play.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
View from inside stationary car showing house clearly visible through windshield. Caleb leans forward eagerly in car seat, clutching dinosaur, asking his impatient question. Cassie twists around with amused, gentle expression to look at little brother. Mum adjusts sunglasses with patient smile. Coconut air freshener sways. Nobody has moved yet, capturing humor of premature "are we there yet."
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Cassie's face transforms from intense concentration to triumphant joy. Her tongue pokes out slightly as fingers fold down methodically, counting by twos. Around her head, glowing mathematical visualizations appear showing house-to-park journeys labeled with distances two, four, six, eight, ten kilometers. Her expression shifts from focused problem-solving to breakthrough understanding, eyes lighting up with accomplishment.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Cassie closes eyes in visualization. Above her head floats dreamy sequence showing car wheel spinning in rotation, leaving glowing trail marked with time and distance increments: one minute equals two kilometers, building to five minutes equals ten kilometers. Trail ends at illustrated Cronulla Beach with sand and waves. Wheel's circumference highlighted by glowing string. Caleb watches sister with wonder, holding dinosaur.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Cassie presses face close to car window, wide curious eyes watching outside world. Through glass, eucalyptus trees, houses, and gardens appear motion-blurred, seeming to rush backward past vehicle. Golden afternoon sun streams through window, illuminating her puzzled, wondering expression. Hand pressed against cool glass. Her face shows fascinating confusion about relative motion while world slides past.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Inside moving car, Cassie catches blue ball with white stars as it drops perfectly back into her raised hands. Her face shows amazement and delighted surprise at unexpected result. Motion lines trace ball's straight up-and-down path. Caleb watches with joy from car seat. Through windows, outside world blurs with forward motion. Mum smiles in rearview mirror. Moment captures scientific "aha" discovery.
</storyboard>

<storyboard>
Family's car parked at Cronulla Beach lot. Children step out onto warm pavement, faces radiating pure joy and excitement. Beautiful beach spreads before them with golden sand, rolling blue-green waves, white foam, distant playing figures. Above children's heads, whimsical sparkling light effects suggest invisible "curious caps." Mum stands beside car with warm, proud smile. Golden afternoon sun bathes everything. Celebration of discovery and adventure.
</storyboard>
