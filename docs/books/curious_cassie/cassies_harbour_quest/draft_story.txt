### Science Words We Learned:

- **Adaptation**: Special features that help an animal survive in its environment
- **Invertebrate**: An animal without a backbone
- **Camouflage**: Colours or patterns that help an animal blend in and hide
- **Symbiotic relationship**: When two different creatures help each other survive
- **Ecosystem**: A community where all living things depend on each other
