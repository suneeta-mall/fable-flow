## Why Community Matters

Creating stories that inspire curiosity isn't a solitary endeavor. **The best educational content emerges from diverse perspectives, shared experiences, and collaborative refinement**. Whether you're just discovering FableFlow or already creating multi-sensory narratives, this community amplifies your impact.

We're building a space where:
- **First-time authors** find encouragement and guidance
- **Experienced creators** share techniques and insights
- **Educators** collaborate on curriculum-aligned content
- **Parents** discover stories that spark family conversations
- **Developers** shape the future of educational technology

## Community Guidelines

We're committed to maintaining a welcoming, inclusive space where everyone feels empowered to contribute. Please review our [community guidelines](guidelines/) to ensure positive, productive interactions.

**Core Principles:**
- **Respect and kindness** in all interactions
- **Constructive feedback** that helps others grow
- **Inclusive language** that welcomes diverse perspectives
- **Focus on learning** rather than criticism
- **Celebrate curiosity** and the joy of discovery
